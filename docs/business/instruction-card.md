## BACK

**Quick Start**

1. Power via USB-C (5V 1A minimum)
2. On your phone/laptop, connect to WiFi: **SprinklerSetup**
3. Open browser → **192.168.4.1**
4. Select your home WiFi and enter password → Save
5. Find board IP in your router's device list (look for "sprinkler")
6. Open browser → **http://[board IP]/**
